What's a CubeSat? 🛰️

A CubeSat is a tiny satellite about the size of a Rubik's cube! These mini satellites are super cool because they're small, affordable, and can do amazing things in space like take pictures of Earth, monitor weather, and even help with internet connections!

Solar Panels
Convert sunlight into electricity to power the satellite
Antenna
Sends and receives signals to/from Earth
Main Body
Contains computer, battery, and scientific instruments

Why are CubeSats awesome?

  • 🎓 Students can build and launch them for school projects!
  • 💰 They cost much less than big satellites
  • 🔬 Perfect for testing new space technologies
  • 🌍 Help scientists study Earth and space
  • 📡 Can work together in groups called "constellations"
